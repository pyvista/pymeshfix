pymeshfix.PyTMesh#

class pymeshfix.PyTMesh(*args, **kwargs)#

Mesh repair and cleaning class.

Wraps the MeshFix Basic_TMesh functionality and exposes it to Python. Provides methods to inspect, repair, and extract mesh data.
